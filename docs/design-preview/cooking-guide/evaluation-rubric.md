# CookPilot S-03 comparison rubric

Score every result from 1 to 5 on the same criteria.

| Criterion | Weight | What to check |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Current action clarity | 25% | “물 500ml를 넣고 끓이세요” is understood within three seconds. |
| Timer legibility | 20% | 02:14 is readable from arm's length and remains visible during AI delays. |
| Voice-state clarity | 20% | Listening, processing, speaking, retry, and off can be distinguished without color alone. |
| Fallback usability | 15% | Previous, replay, +1 minute, and next are obvious and at least 44 px. |
| Kitchen safety and calm | 10% | The screen avoids distraction and makes stop/complete actions deliberate. |
| Product distinctiveness | 10% | The screen feels specific to CookPilot rather than a generic AI app. |

## Experiment arms

1. Shotgun only: breadth of visual exploration.
2. Impeccable only: standalone design-tool interpretation of the same brief.
3. Shotgun → Impeccable: depth added to the selected Shotgun direction.
